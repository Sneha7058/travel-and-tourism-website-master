  • Adhala Dam

Construction of Adhala Dam in Akole taluka is started in 1966. Adhala Dam build on river Adhala in Deothan village. Adhala Dam opened on 1976. Adhala Dam provided water for irrigation for 14-20 villages with 2422 hector area. from Adhala dam there are various drinking water pipelines for nearby villages which include villages like Devthan, Hivargaon Ambre, Ganore, Dongargaon, Pimplegaon etc

Distance from Major Cities:-

  • Nashik - 60 KM
  • Mumbai - 190 KM
  • Akole - 15 KM
  • Sangamner - 25 KM
  • Pune - 166 KM
  • Shirdi - 65 KM

Dam information:-

  • The Type of Build: Earthfill
  • Height: 40.02 meters
  • Construction Start: 1966.
  • Opening date: 1976.
  • Irrigated Area: 2422 hectares.
  • Main Water Reserve: 15.54 Sq
  • Capacity: 1.437 TMC
