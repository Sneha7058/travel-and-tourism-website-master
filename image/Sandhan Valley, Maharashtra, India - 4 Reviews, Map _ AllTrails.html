Sandhan Valley

Moderate•3.5(4)
Kalsubai Harishchandragad Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Photos (4)
Length1.8 miElevation gain173 ftRoute typeLoop

Enjoy this 2.9-km loop trail near Akole, Maharashtra. Generally considered a moderately challenging route. This is a popular trail for backpacking, birding, and camping, but you can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day. The best times to visit this trail are October through May.

Bird watchingCampingHikingWalkingBackpackingForestViewsWildflowersWildlifeScramble
This is a fantastic trail that takes one deep within the Sahyadri Ranges. A lush green forest area with a highly biodiverse environment welcomes one to this location. Porcupines, sambar, and leopards are a few of the animals found in this location, and birds like the white-necked storks and water bees can be spotted over here. Sandhan Valley is often referred to as the shadow valley, and the reason behind it being the shadow valley is that there are very few locations through which light can enter this region. However, this doesn't mean the views are any less beautiful. Rappeling down into the valley is pretty amazing and is an adventure in itself, providing an altogether thrilling experience, along with that, there are options for ziplining as well that can be opted for, although one has to pay for ziplining. It is a magical experience, especially during the winters, thanks to the wide variety of flowers that bloom, providing a colorful background. This trail can be accessed throughout the year except for the monsoon months. Trailhead for this particular trail begins from Shinganwadi Village, which is pretty accessible by roads and can be reached via various different modes of transports such as buses, taxis, cabs, or private vehicles.
